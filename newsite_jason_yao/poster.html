End of Year Show Poster Design

May. 2017

Introduction

The End of Year Show is an annual showcase of works connecting emergent media, computer science, engineering, architecture, electronic music, haptics, and digital art research in Media Arts and Technology Department, UCSB.

The theme of the event this year is "Re-habituation", which expresses an implication of different phases. Inspired by Aristotle's "Four Causes", simulation, digital signal processing, philosophy, electronics are the four aspects used as visual elements in this solution.

Tools: Photoshop, Illustrator, Python, Processing (Java)
