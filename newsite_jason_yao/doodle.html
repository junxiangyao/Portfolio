Doodle in the Air

Apr. 2016

Introduction

Doodle in the Air is a tool for drawing/creating 3D shapes by Microsoft Kinect 1414. In the tracking process, the 3D trajectory of the user's right-hand movement will be saved and displayed. If the two nodes from the trajectory are close enough, they will be connected with a line. Besides, the thickness of the trajectory depends on the speed of the movement. If the velocity is slow, the corresponding trajectory will be thicker.

Advisor: George Legrady

Tools: Processing(Java), Microsoft Kinect 1414

The user is drawing by moving his right hand in the space. The red skeleton indicates the user detected by Kinect, and it can be turned off.


I put a sphere in the virtual 3D space as a virtual button to turn the drawing functionality on (gray) and off (white). While drawing, this sphere could serve as a reference because 3D drawing is displayed on a 2D screen. It can be turned off when the drawing is completed.


The red dot is the cursor indicating the user's right hand while drawing.
