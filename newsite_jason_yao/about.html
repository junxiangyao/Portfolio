My Story

Hi, I am Junxiang Yao. I'm an interaction designer and creative coder based in SF. I believe human-centered design is the answer to utilizing technology in the most empathetic way. In return, technology is a critical reinforcement that enhances the interaction between humans and their life.

I aim to increase people's well-being in designing interaction systematically. I enjoy hunting out root causes and hidden problems in the process of research and design. Thus, reading history books and papers is another hobby of mine due to the same reason: I want to dig out the factors about why things happened the way they did and understand how we are who we are today.

Beyond that, I am passionate about emergent media for interaction especially the ones involve 3D Interfaces. I spend my spare time playing with code, headset, and browsers, and exploring interaction possibilities in immersive environments like VR and AR.
