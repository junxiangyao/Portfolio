My Story

Hi, I am Junxiang Yao. I'm an interaction designer and creative coder based in San Francisco. I believe human-centered design is the answer to utilizing technology in the most empathetic way. In return, technology is a critical reinforcement that enhances the interaction between humans and their life. I aim to increase people's well-being in designing interaction systematically.

Beyond that, I am passionate about emergent interactive media, especially the ones involving 3D Interfaces. I spend my spare time playing with code, headset, data, and browsers, and exploring interaction possibilities in immersive environments like VR and AR.
